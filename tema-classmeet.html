5. Tapi anggota yang ditahan bisa dilepaskan kembali dengan cara teman seanggotanya menyentuhnya.

6. Kelompok di katakan menang apabila dapat menyentuh benteng/tiang lawan.

Jawaban:

i have a best friend. his name is erneston and he is my classmate. we go to school together. erneston comes from an educated family. his father is a school principal and his mother is also a teacher. he is punctual, well-educated, and has good manners. he is really hard working. he always does his homework. he is also well-dressed and well-behaved. all teachers have a high opinion of him. erneston has a well-built body. he is gentle but fearless. moreover, erneston takes part in all sport, scout, trekking and mountaineering activities. he has a good heart and he is truthful. he also plays the guitar, and he makes his parents very proud of him. he secures good marks and is usually top of his class in examinations. he inspires me to work harder. he keeps me away from bad company. i am happy to have such a friend.

kata yang dikoreksi:

1. classmeet -> classmate

classmeet artinya meeting kelas(bukan untuk.mendeskripsikan orang), classmate artinya teman sekelas

2. education -> educated

education artinya pendidikan, educated artinya berpendidikan. karena menceritakan tentang sifat keluarga, educated lebih pantas karena merupakan kata sifat

3. taecher -> teacher (penyebutan yang benar)

4. well-aducated -> well-educated (penyebutan yang benar)

5. taechers -> teachers (penyebutan yang benar)

6. faerlees -> fearless (penyebutan yang benar)

7. quitar -> guitar (penyebutan yang benar)

8. hi -> he (karena menceritakan tentang orang, bukan menyapa..he artinya dia, hi artinya halo)
