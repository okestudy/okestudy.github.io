Dipta : What do you drink, Angga?

Angga : Dragon fruit juice.

Dipta : Dragon fruit? What is that? Is it a real fruit?

Angga : Yes, have you taste it? I think it is really good and refreshing

Dipta : Wow, my God, you are right it is so good

Angga : I told you, the dragon fruit juice is healthy for your body too

Dipta : Yeah, I have heard about it, but I don't know that the taste will be this good

Angga : Yes Dipta, you know what, each dragon fruit are rich of vitamins, including vitamin C, B1, B2 and B3.

Dipta : That is why you drink it?

Angga : Yes.

What is the dialogue talking about?

A. Angga's favorite juice

B. Healthy juice

C. The benefit of dragon fruit

D. Healthy fruit

E. Angga's juice​

Pada soal kita diminta untuk menyatakan apa yang dibicarakan dalam dialog tersebut.
