2. Resep membuat donat dengan caranya​


Bahan:

300 gram tepung terigu protein tinggi

120 gram tepung terigu protein sedang

1 sdt ragi instan

15 gram susu bubuk

40 gram gula halus

1 butir telur ayam

225 ml air es

30 gram margarin

1 sdt garam

Bahan topping:

Coklat batangan yang dilelehkan

Margarin & gula halus

Selai

Cara membuat donat goreng:

1. Campur tepung terigu, susu bubuk, ragi instan dan gula halus. Aduk sampai tercampur rata.

2. Tambahkan telur dan air es sedikit demi sedikit. Jika ragu adonan terlalu encer maka air bisa dimasukkan beberapa bagian dulu.

3. Uleni adonan sampai menjadi kalis. Adonan kalis adalah tidak ada adonan yang lengket menempel di wadah dan tangan.

4. Masukkan margarin dan garam. Uleni adonan sampai elastis.

5. Tutupi adonan menggunakan kain dan diamkan di tempat hangat suhu ruang selama 15 menit. Setelah 15 menit, adonan mengembang. Tinju-tinju adonan untuk mengeluarkan udara. Uleni lagi sebentar.

6. Ulangi langkah 4 tetapi cukup didiamkan selama 10 menit.

7. Tinju-tinju adonan lalu bentuk adonan menjadi lingkaran. Bisa dibentuk manual dengan tangan setelah ditimbang seberat 40 gram atau giling adonan dan cetak memakai cetakan donat.

8. Letakkan adonan yang telah terbentuk dengan diberi jarak pada wadah datar. Tutupi dengan kain dan diamkan selama 30 menit.

9. Goreng adonan donat dalam minyak panas dan api kecil sampai kuning keemasan. Tiriskan.

10. Setelah donat dingin, beri toping sesuai selera.

19. Resep membuat donat dengan bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Mix flour, sugar, instant yeast, baking powder and salt, stirring by hand.

Add egg yolk, knead until it is shaped like fine grains.

Pour liquid milk little by little. Knead until well blended.

Add the butter, knead again in a circular motion, occasionally slam the dough. Do it until the mixture is smooth and smooth, approximately 15 minutes.

Gather the dough together in a basin. Cover with a clean cloth, let stand for 1 hour.

Dough fist until the air comes out and the mixture is flat.

Divide the dough into 30 parts, make a circle like a ball. Let stand again for 10 minutes.

Cut each dough, for best results, do it by hand.

Fried donuts in hot oil using medium-low heat. Fry one part until it turns yellow, then flip until all parts are golden yellow.

Lift, drain and wait for it to cool.

Decorate with topping according to taste.

23. tolong carikan resep donat menggunakan b. Inggris


Simple Donut Recipe
Ingredients:

– 200 cc of water
– 1/2 packs of instant yeast 11 gram
– 1/2 kg of flour
– 4 tablespoons granulated sugar
– 3 tablespoons butter
– 3 tablespoons of powdered milk / condensed milk
– 2 eggs
– 2 teaspoons salt

How to make:

– Mix two eggs with a spoon.
– Enter the flour, sugar, milk, salt, and butter. Mix those materials.
– Put the yeast in the water. Stir it and then put the yeast into the dough.
– Knead until smooth and set aside in a container. Cover tightly and let it for 20 minutes.
– Take 1 tablespoon of dough and round it off. Give the middle hole.
– Do that continuously and put the dough donut you have formed into a big bowl that is coated with flour.
– Fry the donuts dough and let it until cold.
– Sprinkle with powdered sugar or chocolate.
– A simple Donut is ready to serve.

[tex] = 2 \frac{1}{4} + 4 \frac{5}{8} + \frac{1}{2} \\ \\ = \frac{9}{4} + \frac{37}{8} + \frac{1}{2} \\ \\ = \frac{18 + 37 + 4}{8} \\ \\ = \frac{59}{8} \\ \\ = 7 \frac{3}{8} [/tex]

Donat coklat = 32 buah

Donay madu = 30 buah
