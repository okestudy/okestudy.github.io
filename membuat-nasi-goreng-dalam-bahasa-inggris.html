Cara Membuat Nasi Goreng Dalam Bahasa Inggris - Bahasa Indonesia [Procedure Text]

SEKOLAH OKE | Jumat, 23 November 2012

Cara membuat nasi goreng dalam Bahasa Inggris dan Bahasa Indonesia

Dalam Bahasa Inggris

Fried Rice is Indonesia traditional food. It is very popular food. It is easy to make it. It doesn't take too long time to make. The ingredients are easy to get.

How to make Fried Rice? The following is the procedure text how to make fried rice.

Ingredients:

2 cloves of Garlic

2 onions cut into small pieces

1 table spoon of vegetable oil

a plate of rice

some salt

Steps how to make it

First, pound garlic and 2 pinches of salt.

Second, heat vegetable oil on a frying pan.

Third, put the pounded garlic and salt, and onions into the hot vegetable oil. Fry them a while.

Fourth, put one plate of rice. Fry and mix them around 3 menit.

Fifth, add two pinches of salt. Continue frying until the rice is hot enough.

Finally, put the fried rice on a plate. Serve with a sunny-side up.

How to Make Black Pepper Fried Rice

Fried rice is a favorite food of Indonesian people that can be easily processed by yourself because it is only fried. To make fried rice, it only takes a few spices and soy sauce. Besides being easy to make, homemade fried rice is healthier and cheaper. Here's how to make fried rice with a variety of black pepper.

The tools needed are a frying pan and a spatula. Meanwhile, the ingredients needed are rice, cooking oil or butter, salt, and soy sauce. Mashed seasonings: shallots, garlic, red chilies, black pepper, and tomatoes. Other additional ingredients include eggs, beef eyes, meatballs, mustard greens, cabbage, green onions, and fried onions.

Steps:

1. Prepare a frying pan and cooking oil or butter.

2. Blend all the spices until completely smooth.

3. Heat the oil and then add all the ground spices. After the spices give off a fragrant aroma, add additional ingredients such as meatballs, mustard greens, green onions, and cabbage. Mix all ingredients until wilted.

4. Lower heat and then add rice, salt and soy sauce. Mix well.

5. Turn up the heat and let the fried rice heat up.

6. Fried rice ready to be served with beef eye egg and fried onion sprinkles.

It turns out that it's easy not to make black pepper fried rice yourself. Fried rice that is made healthier and more nutritious because it doesn't use micin or MSG. Good luck.

Untuk membuat nasi goreng hanya membutuhkan beberapa bumbu dan kecap. Selain mudah dibuat, nasi goreng buatan sendiri lebih sehat dan murah Berikut adalah cara membuat nasi goreng dengan variasi lada hitam.

        Alat yang dibutuhkan adalah wajan dan spatula. Sedangkan bahan yang dibutuhkan adalah nasi, minyak goreng atau mentega, garam, dan kecap. Bumbu yang dihaluskan: bawang merah, bawang putih, cabai merah, lada hitam, dan tomat. Bahan tambahan lainnya dapat berupa telur mata sapi, bakso, daun sawi, kubis, daun bawang, dan bawang goreng.

Langkah-langkah:

1. Siapkan wajan dan minyak goreng atau mentega.

2. Haluskan semua bumbu hingga benar-benar halus.

3. Panaskan minyak dan kemudian masukkan semua bumbu halus. Setelah bumbu mengeluarkan aroma harum, masukkan bahan tambahan seperti bakso, daun sawi, daun bawang, dan kubis. Campur semua bahan hingga layu.

4. Kecilkan api dan kemudian masukkan nasi, garam, dan kecap. Aduk hingga rata.

5. Besarkan api dan biarkan nasi goreng hingga panas.

6. Nasi goreng siap disajikan dengan telur mata sapi dan taburan bawang goreng.

        Ternyata mudah bukan membuat nasi goreng lada hitam sendiri. Nasi goreng yang dibuat lebih sehat dan bergizi karena tanpa menggunakan micin atau MSG. Selamat mencoba.
