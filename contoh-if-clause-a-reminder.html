12. Contoh kata kata if clause reminder


Jawaban:

Conditional sentences dengan menggunakan if-clause bisa digunakan untuk mengungkapkan antaranya adalah untuk pengingat (reminder). Conditional sentence ini diungkapkan untuk mengingatkan seseorang akan sesuatu yang harus dilakukan jika ingin mendapatkan hasil tertentu. Ini termasuk conditional sentence type 1 yang menggunakan simple present.

If + Simple Present, Subject + Have/Has to/Must + V1

1. If you want to stay healthy, you must eat organic food and vegetables. (jika kamu ingin tetap sehat, kamu harus makan makanan organik dan sayuran)

2. If he wants to be slim, he has to go to a fitness center. (jika dia ingin ramping, dia harus ke pusat kebugaran)

3. If they want to meet she, they must come before 5 pm. (jika mereka ingin bertemu dia, mereka harus datang sebelum jam 5 sore)

21. 10 contoh percakapan if clause reminder, an imperative, suggestion


Jawaban:

Contoh percakapan menggunakan if clause reminder:

A: "Don't forget to bring an umbrella."

B: "Okay, I will bring one in case it rains."

A: "Remember to turn off the lights before you leave the room."

B: "Sure, I will turn them off when I'm done."

A: "Be careful not to spill the coffee."

B: "I will be careful, thanks for the reminder."

Contoh percakapan menggunakan imperative:

A: "Please finish your homework before dinner."

B: "Okay, I will do it right away."

A: "Don't be late for the meeting tomorrow."

B: "I won't be late, I'll make sure to arrive on time."

A: "Don't forget to take your medicine."

B: "Thank you, I won't forget to take it."

Contoh percakapan menggunakan suggestion:

A: "Why don't we try that new restaurant for dinner tonight?"

B: "Sounds like a great idea, let's go!"

A: "Maybe we should take a break and go for a walk outside."

B: "That's a good suggestion, let's do it."

A: "How about we watch a movie tonight instead of going out?"

B: "That sounds like a relaxing evening, let's do it."

A: "Have you considered asking for help from a tutor?"

B: "No, I haven't thought about that. That's a good suggestion, thanks."

25. contoh dialog offering help if clause+reminder


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: conditional clause
Kata kunci: if clause, reminder, offering
Pembahasan:

In English grammar there are several function for conditional clause, they are
- as a reminder: If you wanna be succesfull, you'll have to try harder.
- as a advice: if you have a sudden stomachache, you should rub it with cajuput oil
- as an instruction: if you have a problem with it, ask your teacher now.
- to state the general facts: If you put the ice on the heat, it melts.
- to state the possible act: if it doesn't rain today, we'll go to the beach.

Abedi: Dude, what's wrong? You don't look happy.
Conor: Yeah, sorry , mate, I didn't pass the trial in Arsenal.
A: Oh, what happened?

A: Sorry about that. Well, if you want to be better, you should work on it though.
C: I suppose so. I guess if I have a chance for another trial in the other club, I'll have to learn the club's needs first, what kind of player they need and so on.
A: True, anyway, my uncle's working as a staff in Nantes FC, France, shall I contact him for your trial?
C: Really? yes please. If you have the number now, make a call now, please.That'll be better.
A: Sure, unfortunately, I don't have it now, it's in my house. If I get home, I'll call him soon.
C: Thanks, mate.
A: Just don't overthink any previous facts about British players in France. Just focus on your own.
C: What kind of facts?
A: You know, if the British footballers plays for French clubs, they don't succeed. 
C: No, I don't have that thought.
A: OK, then.I gotta go, I'll text you when I'm done calling.

27. buatlah 5 contoh kalimat if clause + reminder


1. An example of ‘if clause’ + a reminder is: “If you want to pass
the exam, you have to study harder than before.”

2. An example of ‘if clause’ + a suggestion is: “If you want a good
price, why don’t you go to the factory outlet.”

3. An example of ‘if clause’ + a general truth is: “Unless you put
some cherry on it, your cake will look pale and dull.”

4. An example of ‘if clause’ + an imperative is: “If you want to be
the head of OSIS, offer a good program to improve the
school environment.”

5. An example of ‘if clause’ to show a dream is: “If I am elected as
a president, I will waive taxes for poor people.”
