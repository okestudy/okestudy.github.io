Sisi Alas Kerucut yang terpotong

a¹ = 18 , s¹ = 15

a² = ... , s² = 5

Maka Langsung Saja

a²/a¹ = s²/s¹

a²/18 = 5/15

a² = 18 × ⅓

a² = 6 cm

Tinggi Sisi Tegak

tst² = 10² - 6²

tst² = 100 - 36

tst² = 64

tst = √64

tst = 8 cm

Luas Selimut Limas Terpotong

Lp = ( 4/2 )( [ a¹ + a² ] × t )

Lp = 2( [ 18 + 6 ] × 8 )

Lp = 2( 24 × 8 )

Lp = 2( 192 cm² )

Lp = 384 cm² { pilihan 3 }
