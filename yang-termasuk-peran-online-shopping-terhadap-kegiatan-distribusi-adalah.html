⇢៸៸Passenger: No problem.

⇢៸៸Passenger: At what time is it? (Pada pukul berapa?)

⇢៸៸Agent: At 10.00 A.M. (Pukul 10.00 A.M.)

⇢៸៸Passenger: So it’s still an hour later. (Jadi masih satu jam lagi.)

⇢៸៸Agent: Right. Your seat number is 17A. (Benar. Tempat duduk Anda di kursi 17A.)

⇢៸៸Passenger: Thank you. (Terima kasih.)

⇢៸៸Agent: Enjoy your flight, Mr. Antonio. (Nikmati perjalanan Anda, Pak Antonio.)
