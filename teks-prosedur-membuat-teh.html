4. Buatlah teks prosedur membuat minuman dalam bahasa Inggris( kecuali teh & kopi )​


Tools and materials :

• Glass

• Spoon

• Ice cube shaver

• Unflavored soda

• Cocopandan Syrup (can be replaced with other flavors according to taste)

• Vanilla flavored sweetened condensed milk

• Ice

Steps:

• First, pour enough milk into the glass.

• Second, pour enough syrup into the glass.

• Third, pour unflavored soda until it fills of the glass.

• Fourth, stir gently so that the foam from the soda does not come out of the glass until everything is mixed.

• Fifth, shave the ice with an ice cube shaver.

• Sixth, put the shaved ice cubes into a glass containing a mixture of soda, milk, and syrup earlier.

• Seventh, put a little syrup on the ice

• “Es Soda ” is ready to be served.

How to Make Milk Tea

Ingredients:

1. a sachet of instant tea
2. sweet liquid milk
3. water
4. liquid sugar

Steps:

1. Prepare a small pan to boil all of the ingredients.
2. Boil the tea with enough water and stir it until the colour changes into brown
3. Filter the tea and pour it into a cup.
4. Add some sugar and pour the liquid milk as much as you like.
5. Finally, the milk tea is ready to enjoy.

21. contoh teks prosedur pembuatan es teh manis kedalam bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

-> Materials needed:

1. A box of tea

2. Sweet sugar to taste

3. Hot water (if you can boil)

4. Ice cubes to taste.

-> Tools needed:

1.Glass

2 spoon

-> How to make:

1. Prepare a teapot and pour the tea into it according to the dose. If you tend to like teabags that are more practical and effective, you can also choose teabags.

2.Fill the teapot with boiling water. The choice of boiling water is to remove the fragrant aroma from the tea because warm water is not able to maximally produce a fragrant aroma.

3. Wait until the water changes color and add sweetened sugar to it.

4. Stir until the sugar is dissolved and the iced tea turns sweet.

5. Add ice cubes until the heat is defeated by the ice cubes.

6. Let stand a few minutes to cool.

7. Sweet iced tea is ready to drink.

Iced Milk Tea

If you think iced tea is just about lemon slices and ice cubes, try a glass of ice milk for another variant of iced tea. With the pampering milk creaminess, this can be a perfect treatment on a hot day.

How to make:

Boil water.

Add two tea bags in the cup and pour boiled water into it.

Heat half a cup of milk and pour it into a cup.

Add some sugar or honey.

Wait until it gets cooler.

As you wait, prepare a glass ½ until ¾ filled with ice cubes or crushed ice.

Pour your milk tea onto the ice to fill the glass, and enjoy it immediately.
