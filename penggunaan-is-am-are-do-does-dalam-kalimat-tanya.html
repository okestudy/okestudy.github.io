8. pada kalimat simple present tense, bagaimana cara membedakan cara pemakaian am, is, are dan do/does ?​


Cara membedakannya adalah dengan melihat subject untuk setiap kalimatnya.

Yaitu sebagai berikut.

- Subject I menggunakan to be Am.
- Subject She, He, It menggunakan to be Is.
- Subject You, They, We menggunakan subject Are.

______________________________

Lalu do/does digunakan sebagai berikut.

- Do digunakan pada kalimat dengan subject I, You, They, We.
- Does digunakan pada kalimat dengan subject She, He, It.

11. write do/does/am/is/are in the gaps​


AreDoAreDoIsDoesDoAreIsDoesDoIsIsDoDo

Pembahasan

Is, am, are, do dan does merupakan bentuk kata kata kerja dari present simple tense atau biasa disebut to be.

Contoh kalimatnya:

Is = Is she a singer?Am = Am I a college student?Are = Are they at school?Do = Do Sua and Bora have dance class everyday?Does = Does Ms. Lee teach dance class?

Penggunaan:

Penggunaannya bervariasi, tergantung apakah subjeknya jamak atau tunggal dan juga, orang dari kata benda / kata ganti.

"Am" digunakan pada orang pertama tunggal yaitu "I"."Is" digunakan pada orang ketiga tunggal yaitu "he, she, dan it"."Are" digunakan pada orang ketiga jamak atau lebih dari satu orang, yaitu "They, We, Mira and Mera, dll"."Do" digunakan pada subjek plural/jamak yaitu "I, you, we, dan they"."Does" digunakan pada subjek singular/tunggal yaitu "He, she, dan it".

Ket:

Is, Am dan Are biasanya digunakan pada kalimat nominal dari present tense.Sedangkan, Do dan Does biasanya digunakan pada kalimat verbal dari present tense.

Pembahasan Simple Present Tense

Present Tense adalah digunakan utk menyatakan:

Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa sekarang (Present), Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan (habitual) dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Tanda waktu Present TenseEverydayalwaysusuallysometimesoften

Rumus Present Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

kalimat verba yang ada kata kerja (verb) di dalamnya.

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

(-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?

Catatan: Subject + Auxiliary

I, you, we, they + doHe, She, it + does

2. Nominal Sentence

kalimat Nomina yg tidak ada kata kerja di dalam kalimatnya.

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adj/Adv/Noun

(-) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun?

Catatan: Subject + Tobe

I + amYou, we, they + areHe, She, it +’is

Kesimpulan

1. The students do not play at school

=> Para murid tidak bermain di sekolah

Penjelasan:Subject Plural => The student(s) (para murid/murid-murid)Jadi gunakan Do

2. The people do not like the film

=> Orang-orang tidak menyukai film itu

Penjelasan:Subject Plural => People (orang-orang)Jadi gunakan Do

3. The baby cries at night

=> Bayi itu menangis di malam hari

Penjelasan:Subject singular => Baby (Bayi)Maka verb+ies => Cry + ies = cries (menangis)

4. My father is not fat

=> Ayah saya tidak gemuk

Penjelasan:Subject singular => My Father (ayahku)Fat => Adjective (kata sifat)Jadi gunakan Tobe (is)

5. Do you help me?

=> Apakah kamu membantu saya?

Penjelasan:Subject Plural => you Jadi gunakan Auxiliary DO

Simple present tense is a tense which is used to indicate an action that happens in the present. It's also used to indicate something habitual or general truth.
Simple present tense is divided into 2, they are a nominal sentence and verbal sentence. The nominal sentence is a sentence which predicate is a to be, while the verbal sentence is a sentence which predicate is a verb.
Each sentence can be divided into three, they are a positive, negative, and interrogative sentence.

The structure of the verbal sentence:
(+) S + V1 (+s/es) + O
(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O
(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O + ?

The structure of the nominal sentence:
(+) S + to be (am/is/are) + O
(-) S + to be (am/is/are) + not + O
(?) to be (am/is/are) + S + O + ?

Answer:
I will give you 2 examples of verbal sentence and 2 examples of nominal sentence
- Verbal sentence
(+) she buys a book
(-) she doesn't buy a book
(?) Does she buy a book?

(+) They study English
(-) They don't study English
(?) Do they study English?

- Nominal sentence
(+) He is a doctor
(-) He is not a doctor
(?) Is he a doctor?

(+) She is beautiful
(-) She is not beautiful
(?) Is she beautiful?
