3. contoh pengumuman tentang class meeting dalam bahasa inggris


Announcement about class meeting

ANNOUNCEMENT

In order to celebrate Indonesian independence day, class meeting will be held on August 17th 2019.

List of competition:

Band competitionClass decoratingCooking showSoccerBasketballSchool board magazines

All classes must register and send at least one representative for each competition.

Grand prize: Rp 5.000.000,00

For further information and registration please contact Asri at 08927381839 or login to our website https://classmeeting2019.lab.sch

Brace yourself and good luck!

Pembahasan

Announcement adalah jenis teks pengumuman yang membagian informasi mengenai suatu hal kepada banyak orang.

Announcement ada dalam bentuk tertulis maupun lisan. Untuk contoh kali ini adalah written announcement (tertulis) yang ditulis untuk menginformasikan tentang class meeting yang diadakan sekolah dalam rangka hari kemerdekaan RI.

Generic Structure of Announcement

1. Stating Purpose: The text that contains what event will be held

2. Stating Day and Date: Day and Date realization. The text that contains when the event will be held

3. Stating Place: The text that contains where the event will be held

4. Informing Sender: The text that contains name of the person who will be contacted

5. Mana yang benar "Class Leaders" atau " Chairman Class " untuk bahasa inggrisnya ketua kelas?​


"Class Leaders atau Chairman Class"?

Bahasa Inggris dari ketua kelas adalah =

Chairman Class or Class Leader.

Jadi, Chairman Class dan Class Leader sama-sama artinya ketua kelas.

Tetapi lebih cocok dengan

Chairman Class

karena

Chairman=>Ketua

Class=>Kelas

Sedangkan

Class=>Kelas

Leader =>Pemimpin

Jadi yang lebih tepat adalah

CHAIRMAN CLASS

18. buatlah pidato Pembukaan acara osis (bebas. class meeting, parents meeting, dll.) dalam bahasa jawa alus!​


Sugeng rawuh ing acara kita iki, para tamu lan warga sekolah. Kita bakal ngomong babagan osis, kanggo nglakoni tugas kita kanggo nambah kualitas sekolah kita. Osis iku organisasi siswa sing mlebu ing sekolah kita, lan kita duwe tanggung jawab kanggo ngatur, ngkoordinasi, lan nindakake berbagai macem kegiatan kanggo siswa.

Saben dina, kita bakal nerusake tugas kita kanggo ngatur acara-acara penting ing sekolah kita, kaya kelas meeting, parents meeting, lan acara-acara liya. Kita bakal nggawe acara-acara iki kaya ora ana sing liya, kanggo nggawe siswa lan warga sekolah bisa ngerti lan bisa nambah pengetahuan lan pengalaman.

Kita duwe tanggung jawab kanggo nambah kualitas pendidikan ing sekolah kita. Kita bakal nggawe acara-acara sing bermanfaat kanggo siswa, kaya seminar, workshop, lan kegiatan lain sing bisa nggawe siswa bisa berkembang lan bisa ngerti apa sing penting kanggo masa depan.

Mugi-mugi acara iki bisa nggawe kita bisa ngerti apa sing penting kanggo sekolah kita lan bisa nggawe kita bisa nambah semangat kanggo nglakoni tugas kita. Matur nuwun.

23. contoh dialog meeting dalam bahasa Inggris


Here is the example of dialogue in a meeting, in this case, teacher's meeting:

Headmaster: Good morning, fellow teachers, thank you for your time to attend this meeting today. We've got a couple of stuff to discuss, mainly about the students' issue and the how to improve our teaching method in this online class.

Teacher 1: So, what seems to be more crucial one at the moment?

HM: Well, right now, it is the teaching method improvement which is more essential to talk about now.

Teacher 2: I see, well I have some ideas I have tried for my class and it works well.

HM: Sure, well, please do share then.

Teacher 2: I am inspired by the board game. Here is one of them, inspired by chess. You see....

Teacher 1: Ah, interesting, I never see that....
