PEMBAHASANSimple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau. Keterangan yang menunjukkan Past Tense:

YesterdayLast NightTwo days ago

Rumus Past Tense

ada dua yaitu Verbal dan Nominal Sentence

1. Nominal Sentence

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + Adj/Adv/Noun(-) S + Tobe (was/were) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun(?) Tobe (was/were) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun

2. Verbal Sentence

(+) S + Verb 2 + O/C(-) S + did not/didn’t + Verb 1 + O/C(?) did + S + Verb 1 + O/C

=> Subject + TobeI, He, She, it + wasYou, we, they + were

KESIMPULANNo. 1-2-5 Nominal Sentence

1. were - happy - they

=> They were happy

2. interesting - it - was

=> It was interesting

5. she - bored - was

=> She was bored

No. 3-4 Verbal Sentence

3. went - I - to - Bundaran HI - MRT - by

=> I went to Bundaran HI by MRT

4. father - saw - the - news - MRT - TV - on - the

=> Father saw the news on MRT TV
