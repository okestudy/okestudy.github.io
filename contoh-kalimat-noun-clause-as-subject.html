4. buatlah kalimat noun clause as object riding​


Jawaban:

Kalimat berita: I don’t know her book. (Aku tidak tahu bukunya)

Klausa nomina: I don’t know that the book belongs to her. (Aku tidak tahu bahwa buku itu miliknya)

Kalimat berita: He is not sure his aunt. (Dia tidak yakin bibinya)

Klausa nomina: He presume that the woman is his aunt. (Dia menduga wanita itu adalah bibinya)

Kalimat berita: Megan is already married. (Megan sudah menikah)

Klausa nomina: I knew that Megan is already married. (Aku tahu bahwa Megan sudah menikah)

Yes/No questions: Is this her bag? (Apakah ini tasnya)

Klausa nomina: I wonder whether or not this is her bag. (Aku bertanya-tanya apakah ini tasnya atau tidak)

Yes/No questions: Is it yours? (Apakah ini milikmu)

Klausa nomina: I am not sure whether it is yours. (Aku tidak yaking apakah ini milikmu)

Yes/No questions: Are you single? (Apakah kamu lajang)

Klausa nomina: I do not know if you are single or not. (Aku tidak tahu apakah kamu lajang atau tidak)

W-h questions: What is her book about? (Tentang apa bukunya?)

Klausa nomina: I am quite certain to what his book is about. (Aku sangat yakin bukunya tentang apa)

W-h questions: When will she come here? (Kapan dia akan datang ke sini?)

Klausa nomina: Be certain to when she will come here! (Pastikan kapan dia datang ke sini!)

W-h questions: How long do you take bath? (Berapa lama kamu mandi?)

Klausa nomina: Make sure how long you take bath! (Pastikan berapa lama kamu mandi!)

I will go to your house if you need my help. (Aku akan pergi ke rumahmu jika kamu membutuhkan bantuanku)

I heard what he said. (Aku mendengar apa yang dia katakana)

Dini understands why her friend became angry. (Dini mengerti mengapa temannya menjadi marah)

The man knows who broke the car. (Pria itu tahu siapa yang memecahkan mobilnya)

You don’t forget whom she met yesterday. (Kamu tidak lupa siapa yang dia temui kemarin)

The police want to identify whose car he stole. (Polisi ingin mengidentifikasi mobil siapa yang dicurinya)

We don’t know how many books Tiwi reads every day. (Kami tidak tahu berapa buku yang dibaca Tiwi setiap hari)

I don’t know whether I go out with Marnie or with Yoko. (Aku tidak tahu apakah aku pergi dengan Marnie atau dengan Yoko)

14. contoh kalimat noun clause adverbial clause adjective clause


Noun clause 
Example :
1.    I like what she is reading.
2.    She doesn’t know whom she loves more.
3.    I still remember what you did last summer.

Jenis-jenis Adverbial clause antara lain:
1. Clause of Time
- Shut the door before you go out
.- You may begin when(ever) you are ready.
- While he was walking home, he saw an accident.
- By the time I arrive, Alex will have left.

Adjective Clause

1.    I like the book that she is reading.
2.    She doesn’t know the one whom she loves more.
3.    I still remember the killing that you did last summer.
4.    The one whom she loves more will be happy.
5.    The killing that you did last summer will never be forgotten.

1. Noun clause as objects of preposition:

a. You cannot play with someone who aren't interested in a game.

b. I am not interested in whatever you say.

c. She is not responsible for what the girl has done.

d. He is waiting for what his friend is planning.

e. I am really into writing.

2. Noun clause as subject:

a. When she comes is not known yet.

b. When the superhero saves the victim from the danger is the most heroic scene.

c. When he will write is dependent on his mood.

d. Where the monster comes from is still a mystery.

e. Whoever wins that game will be famous.

Pembahasan

Noun clause as object adalah klausa kata benda sebagai objek yang membuatnya bisa berfungsi sebagai layaknya objek biasa dan bisa diganti dengan object pronoun. Begitu pun bila noun clause sebagai subject berarti noun clause tersebut berfungsi sebagai subjek dan bisa diganti dengan subject pronoun biasa.
