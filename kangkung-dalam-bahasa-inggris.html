Jawaban:

Kangkung (Ipomoea aquatica Forsk.) adalah tumbuhan yang termasuk jenis sayur-sayuran dan ditanam sebagai makanan. Kangkung banyak dijual di pasar-pasar. Kangkung banyak terdapat di kawasan Asia dan merupakan tumbuhan yang dapat dijumpai hampir di mana-mana terutama di kawasan berair.

16. Tolong dong buatin cara membuat pelecing kangkung dlm bhsa inggris dan artinya


BAHAN
500 gr Kangkung

1 sdt garam

Sambal Plecing;

7 Buah cabai merah bakar

6 buah cabai rawit merah bakar

1 sdt terasi bakar

8 Butir bawang merah

5 siung bawang putih

2 cm kencur

100 gr tomat merah

2 sdm air jeruk limo

1 sdt garam

1 sdt gula pasir

1 sdt merica

CARA:

-Blansir kangkung dengan cara didihkan air lalu masukkan kangkung dan garam. Masak sampai agak layu. Angkat dan tiriskan.

-Haluskan cabai merah, cabai rawit merah, terasi, bawang putih, bawang merah, kencur, dan tomat merah.

-Bumbui dengan garam, gula, dan air jeruk limo. Aduk rata.

-Sajikan kangkung dengan bumbu



BAHASA INGGRIS:
INGREDIENTS
500 gr Kangkung
1 tsp salt
Sambal Plecing;
7 Red chili grilled
6 grilled red chili fruit
1 tsp of shrimp paste
8 red onion grains
5 garlic cloves
2 cm kencur
100 gr of red tomatoes
2 tablespoons of lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp of sugar
1 tsp pepper

INSTRUCTIONS:

-Turn the water spinach by boiling the water and enter the water spinach and salt. Cook until slightly wilted. Lift and drain.
-Blend red chili, red cayenne pepper, shrimp paste, garlic, onion, kencur, and red tomatoes.
-Season with salt, sugar and lemon juice. Stir well.
-Serve water spinach with herbs

21. Contohkan procedure text tentang penanaman kangkung dalam bahasa Inggris


kamgkung is one of plant in indonesian, the word is "kangkung" taken to sundanese words kang and kung

a. Seed Selection

If you want to make your own seeds below, you need to know:

Seed / candidate seedlings must be certified by the relevant agency if purchased in packaging.

Seed / candidate seeds are fresh, strong, and not perforated or insect bite marks.

Seed / candidate seeds if soaked will sink.

If you want to buy seeds in a shop or at a seedling point, this is something you need to pay attention to:

Make sure the growth is uniformly high between one tree to another not up and down.

Have a lot of roots.

Resistant to the surrounding climate.

Resistant to pests and diseases.

Stems and leaves are sturdy.

b. Planting Stages

Land Kangkung has characteristics:

Grows in a dry place.

Tapered leaves, long, thinner and softer.

The stem is smaller than the water spinach.

White flower color.

How to plant:

c. Land Preparation

bayamorganik. blogspot.com

The first step is to prepare the land by hoeing until the soil becomes loose while removing the remnants of the previous plant roots.

d. Giving Fertilizer

alamtani.com

After the land is finished, the next stage is fertilization carried out before planting the seeds, give organic fertilizer such as cow dung, goats or other. Then the fertilizer is distributed to all parts of the soil, wait 3-5 days for the fertilizer to mix with the soil.

e. Seed Planting

alamtani.com

The right time to start planting is during the rainy season, if you do planting in the dry season watering must be done every day to avoid dryness and end to dead plants.

The distance between plants is 15 to 20cm in one hole containing 3 seeds of a maximum of 5 so that the plants can be more qualified.

f. Stitching

guruilmuanblogspot.co.id

Embroidering is replacing a dead plant and looks less than perfect with new seeds.

g. Care

koranmutiara.com

After all the above processes are carried out the next stage is treatment by giving fertilizer can be done after the plants are 10 days old and do not forget to clean the grass around the plants so as not to interfere with their growth.

h. Harvest

ikiranrakyat.com

Not too long to harvest kale only takes 30 days or one month from the beginning of the planting can be harvested. There are harvesting methods that are uprooted and some are only taken from the base of the stem.

3. How to grow water spinach

water spinach

obatalami.com

Characteristics of Water spinach:

Grow in wet places like rice fields, swamps and other wet places.

Bigger bar.

There is also a rather broad greenish leaf which is brownish red.

Purple and white flowers at the top.

Planting water spinach is easier compared to water spinach. It is proven by the way it is treated which can be said to be simple and easy. Can be planted in the highlands or lowlands as long as there is plenty of water, besides that water spinach has no alias season can be planted at any time.

a. Nursery

Choose quality seeds with sizes from 20 to 30 cm that you can get in stores or buy directly from farmers, usually the farmers use the cuttings method to get the seeds.

b. Land Preparation

The first step is by hijacking the soil so that the texture of the soil becomes splashy, making guludan with a width of 2-3 meters and length according to their respective fields, making the entrance and exit of water, then the water is flooded up to 6 cm before planting let the land first approximately 1 week, after that do fertilization and the land is ready to be planted.

c. Planting

A good planting is done in the afternoon after Ashar, the way to plant it is to stick the seedlings with a distance of about 1.5 x 1.5 cm.

d. Care

The treatment of ckup water spinach cleaning the surrounding weeds is very important to help the growth of the kale.

e. Harvest

It takes a shorter time than ground water spinach, it is enough to wait 11 days from the planting of this water spinach, we can harvest it, so that it can be harvested sustainably leaving the trunk of the tree 2-3 cm so that the kale plants can grow back.

After harvest, wash it first to remove the dirt, if you are clean, you can market it immediately.
