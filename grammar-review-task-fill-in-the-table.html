8. F. GRAMMAR REVIEW Task : Fill in the table. Look back at the dialogs in part D. Pay attention to the italicized expressions and the last two questions following the dialogs. Write down the pattern of expressions to offer a help/ service and its responses. See the example.​


Jawaban:

Pattern: May I ...?

Examples:

May I help you? - Thanks a lot.May I do it for you? - That would be very helpful.May I bring your suitcase? - No, thanks. It's not heavy.

Pattern: What can I ..?

Examples:

What can I do for you? - Can you show me how to use this machine?What can I do to help you? - Please, bring this books to the library. Thanks.What can I read this letter to you? - No, thanks. I can read it myself.

Penjelasan:

Pada soal di atas, kamu diminta mencari ekspresi untuk menawarkan bantuan atau jasa serta responsnya Perhatikan percakapan D, dan kemudian membuat pattern-nya dari kalimat yang berhuruf miring. Kemudian, buatlah contoh kalimat dan tanggapannya dari pattern tersebut.
