Simple Past Tense Positive-Negative-Interrogative with Irregular Verb​:

1. Come

(?) Did my friends come to my house last year?

(-) You didn't give me a book for my birthday.

(?) Did you give me a book for my birthday?

(?) Did he lend me his jacket at that time?

(?) Did he leave me when I'm not there?

Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?

12. Contoh ireguler positif, negatif dan tanya verb 1 dan verb 2


Irregular adalah kata kerja dalam Past tense (V2) dan Past Participle (V3)

Contoh Verb: Tangkap/menangkap

Catch V1 => Caught V2/V3

Contoh Past Tense:

(+) S + V2 + O

=> The cat caught the mouse yesterday

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

=> The cat did not catch the mouse yesterday

(?) did + S + v1 + O?

=> did the cat catch the mouse yesterday?

Catatan:

Pada kalimat Positive (+) menggunakan V2 “caught”

Sedangkan pada kalimat Negative (-) dan interrogative (?) menggunakan V1 “catch” karena sudah ada “did”

15. apa pengertian dari " regular verb dan irregular verb "? Tulislah contoh Regular Verb dan Irregular Verb sebanyak-banyaknya?​


Penjelasan:

regular verb adalah kata kerja beraturan yang di gunakan dalam simple past tense untuk penambahan e dan ed. jika verb berakhiran e maka verb cukup ditambah d. dan apabila verb tidak berakhiran e maka verb ditambah ed.

contoh:

wanted

liked

loved

lived

iregular verb adalah kata kerja yang tidak beraturan yang digunakan dalam simple past tense. untuk ketentuanya bisa dilihat di kamus.

contoh:

saw

took

had

read


16. contoh kalimat irregular verb


Contoh kalimat irregular verb adalah

She wrote a letter yesterday. Mrs. Zaskia has taught English for three years. They made some cookies last night.

PEMBAHASANIRREGULAR VERBS

↪ Adalah kata kerja tidak beraturan. Dikatakan tidak beraturan karena perubahan verb-2 dan verb-3 sudah berbeda dari kata kerja aslinya.

Berikut ini adalah contoh perubahan kata kerja pertama menjadi verb-2 dan verb-3 :

Eat => Ate => EatenDrive => Drove => DrivenBuy => Bought => BoughtGo => Went => GoneDrink => Drank => DrunkSing => Sang => SungSee => Saw => Seen

Penggunaan Irregular verbs dalam kalimat :

Kata kerja kedua (verb-2) digunakan dalam bentuk past tense. Kata kerja ketiga (verb-3) digunakan dalam bentuk perfect tense.

Contoh kalimat past tense :

I ate pizza yesterday. Siska sang a song beautifully. They went to Maluku last month.

Contoh kalimat perfect tense :

I have already seen this movie. She has not bought that house since two years ago.
