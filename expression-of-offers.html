3. Expression Of Offers And Suggestion In Dialogs​


Jawaban:

Expression Of Offers And Suggestion In Dialogs:

A: Hi B, would you like to have some tea?

B: Yes please, that would be nice!

A: Here's your jasmine tea!

B: Thank you, A!

A: By the way, what brings you here to my house, B?

B: Oh, I have a problem. I like this girl and I want to make her feel happy. What do you suggest I do, A?

A: I suggest that you write her a poem. Girls love poems!

B: That is a good idea, A! Thanks!

A: You are welcome, B!

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dalam bentuk percakapan dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Offers (penawaran) dan Suggestion (saran) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Offers:

would you like to have some tea?

Suggestion:

I suggest that you write her a poem.
