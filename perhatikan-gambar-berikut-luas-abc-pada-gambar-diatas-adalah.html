Jawaban:

a.

∆ABC

AB = 12 cm

BC = 9 cm

AC = ?

Gunakanlah rumus Teorema Pythagoras

AC² = AB² + BC²

AC² = 12² + 9²

AC² = 144 + 81

AC² = 225

AC = √225

AC = 15 cm

∆ACD

AC = 15 cm

CD = 25 cm

AD = ?

Teorema Pythagoras :

AD² = CD² - AC²

AD² = 25² - 15²

AD² = 625 - 225

AD² = 400

AD = √400

AD = 20 cm

Luas ∆CAD = 1/2 × AC × AD
