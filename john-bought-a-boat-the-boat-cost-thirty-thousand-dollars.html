Subject : English

Category : Adjective clause

Level : JHS


Adjective clause adalah klausa yang menerangkan kata benda.

Adjective diletakkan mengikuti kata benda yang akan diterangkan.

Adjective clause diawali oleh relative pronouns

that (untuk subject/object orang/bukan orang)

who (untuk subject orang)

whom (untuk object orang)

which (untuk subject/object bukan orang)

whose (untuk kepunyaan orang)

of which (untuk kepunyaan bukan orang)


atau relative adverbs

where (untuk nama tempat; pengganti there) dan when (untuk waktu; pengganti then).

Kalimat kompleks yang mengandung objective clause adalah kalimat yang mengandung relative clause yang menjelaskan objek dari kalimat utama/induknya.

Sesuai aturan relative clause:

jika objek adalah orang, maka kita menggunakan kata ganti who/whom, sedangkan jika objek-nya selain orang, maka kita gunakan kata ganti which/that. Khusus untuk objek yang dijelaskan dengan klausa yang menunjukkan kepemilikian, maka kita gunakan kata ganti whose. Sedangkan objek yang berupa keterangan tempat, kita hubungkan dengan kata ganti where.
Pembahasan

Berikut kalimat-kalimat asal beserta kalimat gabungannya (kata-kata yang menjadi penghubung dicetak tebal):

1. John bought a boat. The boat cost thirty thousand dollars.

John bought a boat which cost thirty thousand dollars.

2. John is the man. We are going to recommend John for the job.

John is the man whom we are going to recommend for the job.

3. The men are angry. The men are in this room.

The men, who are angry, are in this room. (Ini bukan objective clause)

4. The men are angry. I do not like the men.

I do not like the men, who are angry.

5. I do not like the men. The woman is talking to the men.

I do not like the men whom the woman is talking to.The woman is talking to the men whom I do not like.

6. John found a cat. The cat's leg was broken.

John found a cat whose leg was broken.

7. The robber was arrested last night. The police have caught the robber.

The police have caught the robber who was arrested last night.

8. The girl is happy. Her father bought a car for her.

The girl, whose father bought a car for her, is happy. (ini bukan objective clause)

9. This is the city. I was born here.

This is the city where I was born.

10. You put the books somewhere. Show me the place you put them.

Show me the place where you put the books.

adjective clause yang menunjukkan subjek orang menggunakan who, whose, whom
- who apabila subjek pada kedua kalimat sama.
- whom apabila subjek pada kedua kalimat berbeda.
- whose apabila terdapat personal pronoun pada kalimat kedua.

which digunakan pada benda baik pada posisi sebagai subjek maupun objek.

where digunakan untuk menunjukkan tempat.

pada soal-soal ini terdapat beberapa kejanggalan kata yang terkait dengan simple present tense.
bila subjek tunggal (she, he, it), kata kerjanya ditambahkan s/es.
bila subjek jamak (you, we, they), kata kerjanya tanpa s/es.

pada present perfect begitu pula.
subjek tunggal : has + V3
subjek jamak : have + v3

30. 5 The picture shows the Sky Tower in Auckland.Alongside the tower is a boat. The boat is 33 metres long.Use the length of the boat to estimate the height of the Sky Tower


Jawab:

The height of the tower is estimated at 10 × 33 meters = 330 meters.


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Well, we just have a 33 meters boat to estimate the height of the Sky Tower. If you place the 33 meter boat along the height of the Sky Tower, there will be around 10 boats lined up to cover the tower. It means that the height of the tower is equivalent to the length of 10 boats with a length of 33 meters. So the height of the tower is estimated at 10 × 33 meters = 330 meters.
