WHAT IS THE EXPLANATION TEXT'S GENERIC STRUCTURE?

________________________

Jawaban:

Generic Structure of the explanation text :

1. General statement

2. Explanation

Penjelasan:

EXPLANATION TEXT

Explanation text is a text which explains the process ( chronological ) of natural ( phenomenon ) or non natural beyond the human's life. Explanation text is related to How and Why the phenomenon happens.

So, Explanation text menjelaskan proses ( runtutan kejadian ) dari faktor-faktor fenomena alam atau yang bukan termasuk kejadian alam yang terlingkup dalam kehidupan manusia. Explanation text lebih cenderung berkaitan dengan bagaimana dan mengapa fenomena tersebut muncul.

GENERIC STRUCTURE

1. General statement

It is located at the beginning paragraph, it tells the phenomenon that has been issued.

Pada umumnya General statement memaparkan latar belakang dari kejadian -kejadian ( WHY ) yang dipermasalahkan secara global baik berupa fenomena ataupun bukan fenomena alam. Paragraf ini diletakkan di bagian pertama sebagai pembukaan paragraf.

2. Explanation

it tells the sequence process of how the phenomenon occurs.

Paragraf selanjutnya menjelaskan runtutan proses-proses ( HOW ) kejadian  tersebut timbul.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/ COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE

To explain how or why the thing happens.

Untuk menjelaskan bagaimana atau mengapa sesuatu hal terjadi

LANGUAGE FEATURES ( UNSUR KEBAHASAAN )

1. Penggunaan  sequence connection  ( penghubung berurutan )

( first, second, then, ..etc )

2. Penggunaan  passive voice  ( lebih memfokusan tindakan aksi daripada pelaku-pelaku yang terlibat )

( ...is usually caused by.....)

3. Penggunaan  simple present tense ( struktur kalimat menggunakan simple present karena erumuskan kejadian berdasarkan fakta kebenaran secara umum terjadi )

( the water carries along objects .......... )

4. Penggunaan action verbs ( kata kerja verbal ( tindakan aksi )

( makes, live , appear , overflows..........)

5. Penggunaan Noun phrase ( berisi sekumpulan kata-kata yang  memodifikasi satu makna kata benda nya  )

( bad habits , ground surface , lower place, the natural flooding, and etc ......)

6. Penggunaan technical terms ( merujuk pada istilah-istilah ilmiah / teknis  )

( evaporation , condensation .......... )

16. contoh explanation text beserta generic structure?


Tsunami  
The term of “tsunami” comes from the Japanese which means harbour ("tsu") and wave ("nami"). A tsunami is a series of waves generated when water in a lake or a sea is rapidly displaced on a massive scale. A tsunami will be generated when the seafloor abruptly deforms and vertically displaces the overlying water. Such large vertical movements of the earth's crust can occur at plate boundaries. Subduction of earthquakes are particularly effective in generating tsunami, and occur where denser oceanic plates slip under continental plates. As the displaced water mass moves under the influence of gravity to regain its equilibrium, it radiates across the ocean like ripples on a pond. Tsunami always bring great damage. Most of the damage is caused by the huge mass of water behind the initial wave front, as the height of the sea keeps rising fast and floods powerfully into the coastal area.  
Generic Structure   
1. General Statement
The term of “tsunami” comes from the Japanese which means harbour ("tsu") and wave ("nami"). A tsunami is a series of waves generated when water in a lake or a sea is rapidly displaced on a massive scale.  
2. Squenced of Explanation
A tsunami can be generated when the sea floor abruptly deforms and vertically displaces the overlying water. Such large vertical movements of the earth's crust can occur at plate boundaries. Subduction of earthquakes are particularly effective in generating tsunami, and occur where denser oceanic plates slip under continental plates. As the displaced water mass moves under the influence of gravity to regain its equilibrium, it radiates across the ocean like ripples on a pond.  
3. Closing
Tsunami always bring great damage. Most of the damage is caused by the huge mass of water behind the initial wave front, as the height of the sea keeps rising fast and floods powerfully into the coastal area.

18. contoh explanation text rainbow beserta generic structure dan language


Explanation:


Explanation text is a kind of the in which tells about the process or the way something works or is made.


The social function of explanation text:

To explain the process of how something works or how something is made to the readers.


The generic structure of explanation text:

- General statement

This part explains the general introduction of something that will be explained more in the next part.

- Sequenced of explanation

This part contains an explanation of why and how something works or is happened.


Language feature:

- Using simple present tense

- Using chronological connectives, such as first, next, etc.

- Using passive voice

- Using action verbs

Here's the explanation text about rainbow.

Rainbow is a natural phenomenon when an arc of color which is made by light striking water droplets appears in the sky. This phenomenon generally happens after rain. The sequence of rainbow color is always started with red, then followed by orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet.


Rainbow is formed by the refraction of sunlight by water droplets in the atmosphere. First of all, the sunlight passes though the water droplets and it is bent to the center of the droplet. Next, the white light is separated from each other into spectrum of light. The colors that have been separated, then separate again into the very small portions. Actually, the sunlight has several lights beside red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and purple, but our eyes can capture those lights only. Each color is bent at different angles, purple is the color of the first bend while red is the last. Finally those colors form a light arc called a rainbow.


Generic structure:

- General statement = the first paragraph.

- Sequenced of explanation = the second paragraph


Language feature:

- simple present tense

The example which can be found in the text: This phenomenon generally happens after rain.

- chronological connectives

The example which can be found in the text: first of all, next, finally

- passive voice

The example which can be found in the text: Rainbow is formed by the refraction of sunlight by water droplets in the atmosphere.

19. explanation text tsunami beserta generic structure​


A tsunami is a natural disaster that occurs when a large body of water, such as an ocean or sea, experiences a sudden disturbance that causes a series of powerful waves. These waves can cause significant destruction to coastal communities and infrastructure, often resulting in loss of life and property damage.


The generic structure of an explanation text about tsunamis typically includes:

Introduction: This section provides background information about tsunamis, explaining what they are and how they form.
Causes:This section discusses the various factors that can lead to a tsunami, such as earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, and landslides.
Effects: This section describes the impact of a tsunami on coastal areas, including the destruction of buildings, loss of life, and damage to infrastructure.
Prevention and Preparedness: This section offers advice on how to prepare for a tsunami and what to do in the event of an actual tsunami warning.
Conclusion: This section summarizes the key points of the text and reiterates the importance of being aware of the dangers posed by tsunamis.

Overall, the purpose of an explanation text about tsunamis is to educate readers about this natural disaster and provide them with the information they need to stay safe in the event of a tsunami.

How A Cancer Is Formed

What is cancer? It is actually a group of more than one hundred separate diseases. Most of us are fear from cancer It is reasonable because next to heart disease, cancer is the second leading cause of death.


Cancer cells come from normal cells because of mutations of DNA. Those mutations can occur spontaneously. The mutations may be also induced by other factors such as: nuclear and electromagnetic radiation, viruses, bacteria and fungi, parasites, heat, chemicals in the air, water and food, mechanical cell-level injury, free radicals, evolution and ageing of DNA, etc. All such factors can produce mutations that may start cancer.


Cancer cells are formed continuously in the organism. It is estimated that there are about 10,000 cancer cells at any given time in a healthy person. Why do some result in macroscopic-level cancers and some do not? First, not all damaged cells can multiply and many of them die quickly. Second, those which potentially divide and form cancer are effectively destroyed by the mechanisms available to the immune system. Therefore cancer develops if the immune system is not working properly or the amount of cells produced is too great for the immune system to eliminate.

21. write generic structure of explanation text


Jawaban:

1. A general statement

2. A sequence of explanation

3. Closing

Penjelasan:

A General Statement

Yaitu pengenalan suatu peristiwa yang akan kita ceritakan. Atau penjelasan umum tentang peristiwa tersebut. Dalam general statement berisi tentang penjelasan umum tentang fenomena yang akan dibahas, bisa berupa pengenalan fenomena tersebut atau penjelasannya.

A Sequenced of Explanation

Yaitu penjelasan mengapa dan bagaimana peristiwa itu terjadi. Dalam bagian ini penulis dapat menulis lebih dari satu paragraf. A sequenced of explanation berisi tentang penjelasan proses mengapa fenomena tersebut bisa terjadi atau tercipta. A squenced of explanation berupa jawaban dari pertanyaan ‘why’ dan ‘how’ penulis ketika membuat sebuah Explanation text. Dalam squenced of explanation bisa terdiri lebih dari satu paragraf.

Closing

Yaitu penutup atau kesimpulan dari cerita tersebut. Sebenarnya closing itu tidak tercantum dalam generic structure dari Explanation text, tetapi kebanyakan orang beranggapan bahwa paragrap terakhir dari sebuah Explanation text adalah closing, padahal itu merupakan bagian dari sequenced of explantaion yang berisi tentang langkah akhir yang dijelaskan pada bagian squenced of explanation.

Rain is the main source of fresh water for most areas of the world, providing suitable conditions for diverse ecosystems, as well as water for hydroelectric power plants and crop irrigation.

The phenomenon of rain is actually called as a water circle. The concept of the water cycle involves the sun heating the Earth’s surface water and causing the surface water to evaporate. The water vapor rises into the Earth’s atmosphere. The water in the atmosphere cools and condenses into liquid droplets. The droplets grow until they are heavy and fall to the earth as precipitation which can be in the form of rain or snow.

However, not all rain reaches the surface. Some evaporates while falling through dry air. This is called virga, a phenomenon which is often seen in hot, dry desert regions
