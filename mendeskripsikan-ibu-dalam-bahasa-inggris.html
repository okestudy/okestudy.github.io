11. Deskripsikan tentang ibu dalam bahasa inggris dan indonesianya​


║DESCRIPTIVE TEXT║

[tex]Answer[/tex]

I love my mother much. My mother is a great woman. She has the great thinking that I have ever thought. She is a wonder woman. She is a great hero.

Whenever I think of her, she is really great. My mother is a house wife. She can take care of us. She has a great responsibility of caring us and our house. My mother has wavy-long hair,yellow skin, brown eyes and pointed nose. The best thing that I always remember is her great attention for her family.

Translation

Saya sangat mencintai ibu saya. Ibu saya adalah wanita yang hebat. Beliau memiliki pemikiran hebat yang pernah saya pikirkan. Beliau wanita yang luar biasa. Beliau adalah pahlawan yang hebat.

Apa pun yang saya pikirkan tentang dia, beliau benar-benar hebat. Ibu saya adalah seorang ibu rumah tangga. Beliau bisa menjaga kita. Beliau memiliki tanggung jawab besar dalam merawat kita dan rumah kita. Ibu saya memiliki rambut panjang bergelombang, kulit kuning, mata cokelat dan hidung mancung. Hal terbaik yang saya selalu ingat adalah perhatiannya yang besar terhadap keluarga.

[tex]Explanation[/tex]

Descriptive teks merupakan teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE

to describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

1.  Identification  

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di pembukaan awal sebagai perujukkan, pengenalan siapa/apa yang akan dijabarkan dalam teks

2. Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas-nya yang menjadi identik dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

LANGUAGE FEATURES

{ Unsur - unsur kebahasaan yang digunakan }

1. Penggunaan kata benda/hal yang spesifik

Example : my dream, my house, mount merapi, Taj Mahal , Borobudur , my mother

2. Penggunaan  kata sifat ( adjective )

Kata sifat  digunakan dalam penjabaran karakter khusus dari hal yang dibicarakan.

Example : she has a pointed nose, wavy-long hair.

3. Penggunaan pola simple present tense

Secara garis besar, struktur kalimat dalam teksnya menggunakan simple present tense karena sifat informasinya fakta/nyata/apa adanya.

Example : She is really great, She can take care of us.

4. Penggunaan kata kerja aksi

Kata kerja aksi memuat informasi tindakan.

23. tolong deskripsikan tokoh ibu kartini dalam bahasa inggris


Every April 21 people in indonesia commemorate the kartini day. It is beautiful day for the woman because we celebrate the birth of great lady R.A. Kartini. Everyone knows who Kartini is. She is our national heroine and a great lady with the bright idea.

Kartini was born in 1879 April 21 in mayong jepara. Her father was Rama Sosroningrat a Wedana (assistant of head of regency) in mayong. Her mother, Ma Ngasirah was a girl from Teluk Awur village in Jepara as the daughter of a noble family, she felt luck because she got more than the ordinary people got. She got better education than other children. In november 12 1903 she married adipati djoyodiningrat, the head of rembang regency. According to javanese tradition Kartini had to follow her husband. Then she moved to rembang.
 
In September 13 1904 she gave a birth to her son. His name was Singgih. But after giving birth to a son, her condition was getting worse and she finally passed away on september 17 1904 on her 25 years old.

Now Kartini has gone. but her spirit and dream will always be in our heart. Nowadays Indonesian women progress is influenced by kartini's spirit stated on collection of letter "habis gelap terbitlah terang" or  from the dusk to the dawn.
