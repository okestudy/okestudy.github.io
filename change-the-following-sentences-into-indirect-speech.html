13. please change these following sentences into indirect speech​


DIRECT-INDIRECT SPEECH

1) Siti Fatimah said (that) she had not finished her research proposal yet.

2) Aminah says to Aisyah (that) she will go to her house after she finishes her work.

3) Jatmiko said (that) where his tie was. He had put there the day before.

4) Sintawati said to Mother (that) she wanted to buy a new dress so that her friends looked her beautifully on her birthday party.

5) Peter asked to Father whether he could help him to study that lesson. He would do the test the next day.

6) Peter said (that) he had been watching TV when Ani had called him.

7) Wati said to Shinta (that) her computer was broken by her brother. She had to repair it.

8) Wati asked to Ann whether she knew the man who stood there.

9) Morher asked to her son whether he had finished his research proposal.

10) Teacher ordered the students to do that exercise.

EXPLANATION

Direct speech merupakan pernyataan yang disampaikan langsung oleh narasumber. Direct speech terpapar dalam bentuk percakapan dengan disertai tanda petik ganda ("). Sedangkan, Reported speech (indirect speech), merupakan pemberitaan ulang pernyataan yg disampaikan oleh narasumber kepada orang lain di kurun waktu berbeda dengan menggunakan bahasa sendiri, tanpa mengubah informasi yg tersirat.

Perubahan bentuk noun clause verb pada reported speech bergantung pada bentuk reporting verb dan jenis informasi yg disampaikan pada direct speech, apakah berupa informasi faktual, imperative, interrogative dan prohibition.

Jika reporting verb berbentuk present {says, tells, asks}, noun clause verb tidak mengalami perubahan.

Sedangkan, jika reporting verb berbentuk past {said, told, asked}, noun clause verb mengalami backshift berikut ini :

Verb tense

➻Simple present ➛Simple past

➻Simple past ➛Past perfect

➻Present perfect ➛Past perfect

➻Simple future ➛ Present conditional

➻Present continuous ➛Past continuous

➻Past continuous ➛Past perfect continuous

Adverb of time

➻ Now ➛at that time

➻ Today ➛ that day

➻ Yesterday ➛ the day before

➻ Tomorrow ➛ the next day

➻ Last week ➛ the previous week

Perubahan ini tidak berlaku, jika yg dibahas adalah kebenaran umum (general truth). Meskipun, reporting verb-nya bentuk lampau, noun clause verb-nya akan tetap sama berbentuk simple present karena yg diulas adalah kebenaran/fenomena.

Yang perlu diperhatikan, dalam merubah direct speech ke indirect speech, yaitu :

1) Jenis reporting verb yg digunakan, berbentuk present atau past.

2) Jenis informasi yg disampaikan, apakah berupa imperative, interrogative, exclamation.

3) Pronoun (kata ganti orang), posisi orang yang terlibat.

4) Verb tense

5) Adverb of time (keterangan waktu)

6) Adverb of place (keterangan tempat/petunjuk)

15. perintahnya : change the following sentences into indirect speach


Explanation:


-Indirect speech for statement-

English has two ways to narrate the spoken-words of a person, they are:

- Direct speech is reporting what someone else said or wrote by using the actual words of the speaker or writer.

For example, Jenny said, “I went to Bali yesterday”


- Indirect speech, which is also known as reported speech, is reporting what someone else said or wrote but not using the same words as what the person said or wrote.For example, Jenny said that she had gone to Bali the previous day.


 

How to change direct speech into indirect speech?

There are some rules to do that:1. Indirect speech doesn’t put the words of the speaker in quotation marks.

2. Indirect always use the conjunction “that”. This conjunction is always used between reporting verb and reported speech.For example:

Direct speech: Jenny said, “I went to Bali yesterday”

Indirect speech: Jenny said that she had gone to Bali the previous day.


3. If the reporting verb is either present tense or future tense, the tense of the reported speech will not change. But, if the reporting verb is past tense, the tense of the reported speech will change.For example:

Direct speech: Jenny says, “I went to Bali yesterday”

Indirect speech: Jenny says that she went to Bali the previous day. (there’s no tense changing)


For example:

Direct speech: Jenny said, “I went to Bali yesterday”Indirect speech: Jenny said that she had gone to Bali the previous day. (tense changed)


4. The pronoun will be changed.For example:

Direct speech: Jenny said, “I went to Bali yesterday”

Indirect speech: Jenny said that she had gone to Bali the previous day.


5. If there’s time signal mentioned in the reported speech it will be changed in indirect speech. The example of time changing: now into then, today into that day, yesterday into the previous day, and tomorrow into the next day.For example:

Direct speech: Jenny said, “I went to Bali yesterday”

Indirect speech: Jenny said that she had gone to Bali the previous day.


-Indirect speech for question sentences-

The rules of indirect speech for statement will still be used along with some additional rules below (for yes or no question):

1. The question mark will be removed.


2. In indirect speech, the question sentence will be expressed in positive form instead of interrogative form.

For example:

Direct speech: She said to me, "are you tired?"

Indirect speech: She asked me if I was tired.


3. The reported verb, such as say or tell in direct speech will be replaced with verb, such as ask or wonder in indirect speech.

For example:

Direct speech: He said to me, "do you know her?"

Indirect speech: He wondered if i knew her


4. The conjunction "that" will not be used in yes or no question indirect speech. We will use "if" or "whether" instead. If or whether can be used interchangeably.

For example:

Direct speech: John said to her, "do you like tea?"

Indirect speech: John asked her whether she liked tea


For 5W + 1H question, the rules are same. The only difference is in 5W + 1H question the word "if" or "whether" will not be used.

For example:

Direct speech: She said, "How is your condition?"

Indirect speech: She wanted to know how my condition was.

29. Task 11Change the following into indirect speech!​


Direct Speech atau kalimat langsung adalah kalimat menirukan ucapan seseorang persis seperti apa yang dikatakan orang tersebut. Ketika kita menulis menggunakan kalimat langsung, kita menggunakan tanda baca (" ") dan tidak ada perubahan bentuk kalimat.

Contoh:

She says, "Where do you want to go?"She said, "Where do you want to go?" Pembahasan

Lalu, bagaimana dengan indirect speech?

Indirect Speech atau kalimat tak langsung adalah kalimat yang biasanya digunakan untuk menceritakan masa lalu, sehingga ada perubahan bentuk kalimat. Biasanya kita menggunakan kata 'that'.

Contoh:

She said, "I saw him." diubah menjadi She said that she had seen him.

Perubahan dari direct speech ke indirect speech

Present Simple Tense → Past Simple TensePresent Continuous Tense → Past Continuous TensePresent Perfect Tense → Past Perfect TensePresent Perfect Continuous Tense → Past Perfect Continuous Tense Past Simple Tense → Past Perfect TensePast Continuous Tense → Perfect Continuous TensePast Perfect Tense (tidak berubah)Past Perfect Continuous Tense (tidak berubah)Future Simple Tense → “would”Future Continuous Tense → “would be”Future Perfect Tense → “would have”Future Perfect Continuous Tense → “would have been”

"Which way did he go?" Asked the young woman.

     2. My father said to me, "where are you going?"

Present Continuous Tense → Past Continuous Tense

The answer: My father said to me where I was going.

Command, insert to + infinitive

The answer: He told his sister to wait her till she return.

Change 'Said' with 'Suggested' and ‘Let’ with ‘should’

Present Simple Tense → Past Simple Tense

The answer: Denias Pratama said how clever he was.

The answer: He said that he had been studying since seven o'clock
