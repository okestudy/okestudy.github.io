Nation animal called( order) Testudinata( or Chelonians) This typical and easily recognizable in the presence of'rumah' or shell( bony shell) the hard and rigid. Shell Turtle consists of two parts. The top of the cover back called the carapace( carapace) and the bottom( ventral, stomach) called plastron. Then every part consists of two layers. outer layer generally form of scales large and hard, and arranged such as precarious; while the layers of the inside the form of plates bone compact like shell. The exception contained in the group turtles- turtles( Trionychoidea) and type of leatherback turtles, which layers of its outer no scaly and replaced the skin layer on the outside shell bones. In the Indonesian, we know the three groups of animals including the nation, yalah turtle( English: sea turtles), turtles- turtles or struck( freshwater turtles), and turtles( tortoises). 25. deskripsikan hewan kura kura dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan kata sifat


Mendeskripsikan hewan kura-kura dalam bahasa Inggris menggunakan kata sifat adalah sebagai berikut:


Turtles are reptiles well known for their hard shells that protect them from predators. Turtles live in majority part of the world in almost every climate.

There are many different types of turtle so there is not average size for them. You could find the largest size which is the leatherback turtle. They are very heavy and could weigh up to 680 kilograms. But there are also small ones, they’re called Cape tortoise. The lightest is about 142 grams only!

Turtles are very adaptive animals that is why you can always find them in almost every part of the continent. However, turtles are not sociable. They don’t interact much like other animals do. Turtles are omnivores, it means that they eat various things.


Pembahasan

Untuk mendeskripsikan hewan kita perlu mengetahui apa saja kata-kata sifat yang umum digunakan. Sebagai contoh untuk kura-kura kakak menggunakan kata old karena kura-kura termasuk dalam salah satu binatang tertua. Juga heavy dan light, karena mereka memiliki berat yang bervariasi tergantung spesies.


Kata sifat lain yang juga menggambarkan personality dari kura-kura adalah adaptive yang artinya mudah beradapasi. Not sociable, artinya tidak mudah bersosialisasi dan omnivores artinya pemakan segala jenis makanan.

26. Contoh deskripsi kura kura dengan bahasa inggris . Identification dan description


A turtle is an amphibian animal that lives in the water and on land.
The shell of the turtle is what makes it unique. The top domed part of a turtle’s shell is called the carapase and the bottom underlying part is called the plastron. The turtle’s shell also has nerve endings in it. If you touch the shell, a turtle can feel it. Turtles pull their heads and feet into their shells to protect themselves. Some Box turtles can eat so much that they no longer will fit into their shell.
All turtles lay their eggs on land. When in the eggs, turtles take about 2 months to incubate and their sex is determined by the temperature. Under 29 degrees they become male, over 29 degrees they become female.
