3. contoh personal pronouns subjective dan adjective​


Jawaban:

Personal Pronouns subjective

I (saya)

I will go to the US next year (Saya akan pergi ke Amerika Serikat tahun depan)

You (kamu)

You are really talented! (Kamu sangat berbakat!)

She (dia – perempuan)

She has the most beautiful round eyes (Dia memiliki mata bundar yang paling indah)

He (dia – laki-laki)

He can speak English fluently (Dia dapat berbicara dalam bahasa Inggris dengan lancar)

They (mereka)

They are my neighbors (Mereka adalah tetanggaku)

We (kita, kami)

We are going to the movies (Kami akan pergi ke bioskop)

It (dia, itu – benda, binatang)

Dan Adjective

my our

you your

his

her their

its

16. Write five sentences using subjective,objective pronouns and possessive pronouns


Pronoun is a word that takes the place of a noun, such as: I, you, me, it, they, we, she, him, us. Pronouns are frequently used to prevent unimportant repetition. There are several types of pronouns: subjective, objective, possessive adjectives, and possessive pronouns.

Pembahasan

Five sentences using subjective pronouns are

I have a cat.He sleeps on the sofa every night.She buys flowers everyday.They play football in the yard.We study in the classroom.

Five sentences using objective pronouns are

My mother gives me some money to buy stationeries.My father is looking for his car key. I see it on the table in the living room.Budi is my classmate. I sit next to him in the garden.Mary is a beautiful girl. All the boys like her very much.He lives far way from his family. He writes a letter to them every week.

Five sentences using possessive adjectives are

I have a cat. This is my cat.We study in vocational school. Our school is near traditional market.He loves reading books. But, he doesn't have his own books. He borrows them from his friends.She is studying abroad now. She always misses her family. They play football in the school yard. But they always play it with our ball.

Five sentences using possessive pronouns are
