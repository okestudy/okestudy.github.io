1. cara menggunakan simple present do and does


kalau tobe do itu untuk subject plural.
subject plural = i,you,they,we
example :
do you usually eat breakfast every morning?

klo tobe does itu untuk subject singular
subject singular = she,he,it
example:
does rani always go to class every day?

Emily is a busy high school student. She wakes up at 6:30 in the morning to get ready for school. She takes a shower, brushes her teeth, and gets dressed. Emily doesn't have time to eat breakfast at home, so she grabs a granola bar on her way out the door.

Emily's first class is math, and she does well in the class. She always pays attention in class and does her homework. After math, Emily has science and then history. She enjoys both of these classes, but science is her favorite.

MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI : SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE
KELAS : 7
KODE SOAL : 7.5
KODE KATEGORISASI : 7.5.1

SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN
==========================
Rumus dalam SIMPLE PRESENT tense yang digunakan adalah :
(+) subject + V1 (s/es) + object
(-) subject + do/does + not + V1 + object
(?) do/does + subject + V1 + object

Catatan!!!
* Tambahan s/es pada kalimat positif hanya untuk subject she/he/it/nama benda singular.
* Tambahan DO pada kalimat negatif dan tanya digunakan untuk subject I/you/they/we/nama benda plural.
* Tambahan DOES pada kalimat negatif dan tanya digunakan untuk subject she/he/it/nama benda singular.

Jadi jawabannya adalah sbb :
1). (+) Adila and Sarah have some homework from school every weekend.
(-) Adila and Sarah (do not have) some homework from school every weekend.
(?) (Do Adila and Sarah have) some homework from school every weekend?


2). (+) The students and some teachers visit some historical places every month.
(-) The students and some teachers (do not visit) some historical places every month.
(?) (Do The students and some teachers visit) some historical places every month?



3). (+) The teacher teaches the students in the classroom.
(-) The teacher (does not teach) the students in the classroom
(?) (Does The teacher teach) the students in the classroom?


4). (-) Father (does not drive) his car to his office every Wednesday.
(+) Father (drives) his car to his office every Wednesday.
(?) (Does Father drive his car to his office every Wednesday?

5). (-) Mrs. Aline (doesn't want) the delicious food every evening.
(+) Mrs. Aline (wants) the delicious food every evening.
(?) (Does Mrs. Aline wants) the delicious food every evening?
