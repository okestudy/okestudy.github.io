In translating words or phrases from Indonesian to English or vice versa, you need to use the proper words for the target language and adjust it with the appropriate register and context.
The proper translation for the phrase 'pura-pura bahagia' is pretending to be happy (if as a gerund) or as a verb it can be 'pretend to be happy'. Here is the example in the sentence
