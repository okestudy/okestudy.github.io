Total Nasabah = 80+105+70+85+75 = 415
Senin = 80/415 × 100 = 19%
Selasa = 105/415 × 100 = 25%
Rabu = 70/415 × 100 = 17%
Kamis = 85/415 × 100 = 20,5%
Jumat = 75/415 × 100 = 18%
