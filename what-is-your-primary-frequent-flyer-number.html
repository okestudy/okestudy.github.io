17. What is the difference between flyer, pamphlet, and newsletter?​


Jawaban:

Pamflet dan brosur memiliki ukuran yang sama yaitu selembaran kertas folio atau A4. Tapi, biasanya brosur dapat dilipat atau dapat dijilid. Pamflet adalah selembar kertas yang dikemas dengan cara dilipat yang saling berhubungan satu sama lain dan tidak memiliki cover serta tidak dijilid.

Penjelasan:

b.ing ver

Pamphlets and brochures have the same size, namely a sheet of folio or A4 paper. However, usually brochures can be folded or can be bound. A pamphlet is a sheet of paper that is packaged in a folded manner that is interconnected with one another and has no cover and is not bound.
